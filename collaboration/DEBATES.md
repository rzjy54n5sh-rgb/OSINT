# MENA Intel Desk — Structured Disagreements

> When either analyst believes a position deserves formal challenge, open an entry here.  
> This is not conflict — it is the mechanism that prevents groupthink and keeps analysis honest.  
> Omar makes the final call on all analytical disputes. Log the outcome in DECISIONS.md.

---

## How to Open a Debate

1. Create a new entry with the template below
2. State your position, evidence, and probability/conclusion clearly
3. Tag the other person in IDEAS.md: "[Name] — debate open in DEBATES.md: [Topic]"
4. The other person responds in the "Counter Position" section
5. Both Claude instances read open debates and can stress-test both sides
6. Omar closes the debate with a ruling and logs it in DECISIONS.md
